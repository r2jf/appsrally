Valid Entries
Windows 8/Windows Phone 8:
A valid entry is an implementation of Windows 8 or Windows Phone 8 published in the Windows Store after August 23, 2013 from one of the participating countries to reach or exceed 100 downloads before December 7, 2013 and is reported to AppsRally@microsoft.com prior to that date. You can register one or more Apps!


Windows Azure:
A valid entry is an application built on one of the following languages​​:. NET, Node.js, Java, PHP, Python, Ruby. Published in an Azure account non-trial, pay-per-use or Bizspark and is reported to AppsRally@microsoft.com before December 7, 2013. You can register one or more Apps!
How do I enter the contest?
Build, publish and promote your app between the start and end dates of the contest, when your app downloads exceed 100 downloads (Windows Phone or Windows) or when you post your application in the cloud (Windows Azure) email us at AppsRally@microsoft.com with your name, country, e-mail that you used to register in the store or in Azure, link and name of your application, a link to a short video where you explain the main features of it, and one screenshot where we see that your app downloads are above 100. The judges will see your video and evaluate your application.
Contest dates
The contest starts on August 23, 2013 and ends on December 7, 2013
